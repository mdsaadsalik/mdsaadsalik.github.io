About Me

I am a passionate Robotics and AI researcher with a Master's in Machine Design from Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, and a Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering from Savitribai Phule Pune University. My expertise bridges the gap between mechanical engineering fundamentals and cutting-edge AI technologies.

My research focuses on revolutionizing human-robot interaction through innovative haptic feedback systems, gesture recognition, and advanced control algorithms. I specialize in applying deep reinforcement learning and differentiable inverse kinematics to solve complex robotic manipulation challenges.

I have developed and implemented the HiPPO-DIK framework for high-DOF robotic manipulation within ROS environments, demonstrating practical applications of AI in robotics. My work spans from theoretical research to real-world implementations, with successful projects in both academic and industrial settings.

With professional experience as a CAE Engineer and Research Assistant, I bring a unique combination of theoretical knowledge and practical engineering skills. I'm passionate about pushing the boundaries of what's possible in robotics and AI, and I'm always excited to collaborate on innovative projects that shape the future of technology.
