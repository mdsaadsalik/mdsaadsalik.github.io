Professional CAE Analysis & Engineering Simulation Services

I provide comprehensive Computer-Aided Engineering analysis services that ensure your designs meet performance, safety, and reliability standards. Using advanced simulation tools and methodologies, I deliver accurate predictions of product behavior under real-world conditions, enabling data-driven design decisions and optimization.

Comprehensive CAE Services

Finite Element Analysis (FEA)

Structural, thermal, and modal analysis using advanced FEA techniques to predict product performance and identify potential failure points.

Static & Dynamic Analysis

Linear and non-linear stress analysis for both static loads and dynamic conditions including impact, vibration, and fatigue.

Structural Optimization

Topology, shape, and size optimization to reduce weight, improve performance, and minimize material usage while maintaining structural integrity.

Fatigue & Durability Analysis

Life cycle predictions and durability assessment under cyclic loading conditions using advanced fatigue analysis methodologies.

Thermal Analysis

Heat transfer simulations, thermal stress analysis, and cooling system optimization for thermal management applications.

Validation & Correlation

Correlation of simulation results with experimental data and validation against industry standards and regulatory requirements.
