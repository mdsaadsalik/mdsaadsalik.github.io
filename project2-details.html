Academic Project | M-Tech Machine Design | 2023

Project Overview

This academic project involved the comprehensive design, simulation, and optimization of a 3-DOF articulated robotic arm specifically tailored for industrial applications. The focus was on achieving high precision, operational speed, and reliability in manufacturing environments through advanced kinematic modeling and control strategies.

Key Features & Design Aspects

Kinematic Analysis

Complete forward and inverse kinematic models developed for precise motion planning and trajectory optimization.

Dynamic Modeling

Detailed dynamic models created using MATLAB Simulink to simulate real-world performance under various loading conditions.

Torque Control Strategy

Advanced torque control implementation for accurate end-effector positioning and smooth operation.

Structural Optimization

Weight reduction and performance enhancement through finite element analysis and material selection.
